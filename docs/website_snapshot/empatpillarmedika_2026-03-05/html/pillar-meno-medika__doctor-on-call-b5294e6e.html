Doctor On Call Services

24 Hours Doctor On Call

On-call doctor services are available via voice or video phone. Pillar Meno Medika provides this service if the patient's condition warrants a hotel visit. The patient requests that our medical personnel come to the hotel to do the essential checkup. This is done for the patient's convenience because there are no vehicles on Gili Meno, making it difficult for the patient. Please visit Pillar Meno Medika for treatment.
